Defeat Poverty in Africa (IFT 458 Project 1) - History

"Defeating Poverty One Meal At A Time!"

History

We were founded in 1998 by a group of community activists who wanted to make a difference in the world in particular through alleviating poverty in Africa. This would be done through giving out meals to the needy of Africa and funded entirely by private donations and corporate donations in kind.
